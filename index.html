About Me

Hello! I'm a Software Developer from New York City looking to showcase some of the projects I've worked on. I hope they will inspire someone to go out, and create because that's what it's all about. I'm always challenging “the usual”, constantly coming up with new ideas on how things could be better. To me, inspiration comes from re-imagining what you’re used to. While working on these projects I've found myself venturing into unknown fields trying to bring my ideas to life. Being in that mindset of always learning something new has taught me skills that I didn't even know I would be interested in. I encourage you to get started on that project you’ve been thinking of, and as always, stay inspired! - Dan
